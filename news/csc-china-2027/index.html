Chinese Government Scholarship 2027 — the window usually opens in winter

deadline 16 July 2026 · by Maqsudjon Polatov

Key facts

  • Covers: tuition + dormitory + monthly stipend + insurance
  • Two routes: embassy track and university track
  • Applications for 2027 typically run late 2026 → spring 2027 (varies by route)
  • Many programmes are English-taught — IELTS strengthens the file; Chinese-taught ones add a language year

The Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) is one of the widest doors to funded study abroad: bachelor's, master's and PhD across hundreds of Chinese universities, with tuition, dormitory and a monthly stipend covered. Applications go either through the Chinese Embassy in Tashkent or directly to a university — and the window for the 2027 intake typically opens in winter and closes in spring.

Use the autumn well

  1. Shortlist universities and programmes on campuschina.org — filter by English-taught if you don't speak Chinese.
  2. Prepare notarised translations of your diploma and transcript; they take weeks, not days.
  3. An IELTS certificate is often optional but consistently strengthens embassy-track files.
