Computer IELTS results arrive in 1–5 days — paper takes about 13

exam update 16 July 2026 · by Maqsudjon Polatov

Key facts

  • Computer-delivered results: 1–5 days
  • Paper-based results: about 13 days
  • Same test, same scoring — only the delivery differs
  • Tight scholarship deadline? The gap can save your application

Both formats are the same exam with the same band scale — but the results timeline is not the same. Computer-delivered IELTS results are typically released within 1–5 days; paper-based takes around 13 days.

When this matters

Chasing a deadline — a university CAS, a scholarship document window, a DTM submission date? Count backwards: exam date + result days + a safety margin. On a tight calendar the computer format effectively buys you an extra week. If you type slower than you write, weigh that against the Writing section before booking.
