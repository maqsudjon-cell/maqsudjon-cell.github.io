QS 2027 rankings are out — check your university before the next El-Yurt Umidi call

deadline 16 July 2026 · by Maqsudjon Polatov

Key facts

  • QS 2027 edition released in June 2026 at topuniversities.com
  • El-Yurt Umidi funds universities from the QS / THE top lists
  • A university can drop out of range between calls — re-check yours
  • THE World University Rankings 2027 lands in autumn

Every summer QS refreshes its World University Rankings — and for anyone planning an El-Yurt Umidi application, this is not trivia. The foundation funds study at top-ranked universities, and rankings move: a university that qualified last year can slip out of range, and one that didn't can slip in.

What to do this week

  1. Open the QS 2027 list and find your target universities' new positions.
  2. If a target dropped, add one or two backup universities that are safely inside the ranking range.
  3. Remember the real deadline is admission — an acceptance letter takes 2–6 months, the foundation's window is only ~3 weeks.
