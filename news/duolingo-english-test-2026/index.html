Duolingo English Test: 5,500+ institutions accept it — but know its limits

exam update 16 July 2026 · by Maqsudjon Polatov

Key facts

  • Accepted by 5,500+ institutions worldwide (mostly US-leaning)
  • About $65, ~1 hour, taken at home with a webcam
  • Not valid for UK visa (SELT) routes; many scholarships still specify IELTS
  • Results in ~2 days

The Duolingo English Test has become a real budget alternative: about $65, roughly an hour, taken from home, results in around two days, and an acceptance list that has grown past 5,500 institutions — strongest in the US.

Where it does not work

  1. UK visa routes that require a SELT — DET is not on the approved list.
  2. Government scholarships (El-Yurt Umidi, Chevening universities, most state programmes) overwhelmingly specify IELTS or TOEFL.
  3. Always check the exact university page — "accepts DET" for admission does not always mean "accepts DET for the scholarship".

Strategy: DET is a great low-cost backup for US applications; IELTS remains the universal key for scholarships and the UK.
