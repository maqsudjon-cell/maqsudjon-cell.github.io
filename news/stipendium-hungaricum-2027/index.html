Stipendium Hungaricum 2027/28 opens around November — prepare now

deadline 15 July 2026 · by Maqsudjon Polatov

Key facts

  • Expected opening: mid-November 2026 (same rhythm every year)
  • Expected deadline: mid-January 2027 — last cycle closed 15 January, 2 p.m. CET
  • Covers: tuition + accommodation support + monthly stipend + health insurance
  • Levels: bachelor's, master's, one-tier master's, PhD · Uzbekistan is eligible

Stipendium Hungaricum — Hungary's fully funded state scholarship — is one of the most popular routes to Europe for Uzbek students, and its calendar barely moves from year to year: the portal opens in mid-November and closes in mid-January. The 2026/27 cycle closed on 15 January 2026 at 2 p.m. CET; expect the 2027/28 call to follow the same schedule.

Why start in July if it opens in November?

Because the two things that decide your application take months, not days:

  1. English certificate. Most Hungarian programmes ask for proof of English — many list IELTS around 5.5–6.5 depending on the programme. A test taken in September–October lands the certificate exactly in time for the application window.
  2. Motivation letter and documents. Transcript translations, recommendation letters and a motivation letter tailored to your two programme choices always take longer than planned — start collecting them in autumn.

How the application works

  • Apply online at apply.stipendiumhungaricum.hu — you can pick up to two study programmes.
  • In Uzbekistan the nomination goes through the national sending partner (follow the Ministry's announcement when the call opens).
  • After nomination: university entrance exams/interviews in spring, results by summer 2027.

🇺🇿 Qisqacha

Vengriyaning to'liq granti (o'qish + yotoqxona + stipendiya + sug'urta) har yili bir xil jadvalda: ariza noyabr o'rtasida ochilib, yanvar o'rtasida yopiladi. O'tgan sikl 15-yanvarda yopilgan. Hozirdan qilinadigan ishlar: IELTS'ni sentabr–oktabrda topshirib sertifikatni tayyorlab qo'yish (ko'p dasturlar 5.5–6.5 so'raydi), hujjat tarjimalari va motivatsion xatni kuzda yig'ish. Ariza: apply.stipendiumhungaricum.hu.
