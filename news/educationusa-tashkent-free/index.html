EducationUSA Tashkent: free, official help for US university applications

resource 16 July 2026 · by Maqsudjon Polatov

Key facts

  • Free official advising — funded by the US State Department
  • Guidance on admissions, essays, financial aid and scholarships
  • Opportunity Funds: covers test and application fees for selected low-income students
  • Regular free info-sessions in Tashkent and online

Paying an agency for US admissions advice is often unnecessary: EducationUSA — the US State Department's official advising network — runs a Tashkent centre with free advising sessions, essay and application guidance, and university fairs. It is the primary official channel for anyone aiming at US bachelor's or master's study.

Best-kept secret: Opportunity Funds

For high-potential students with limited means, the Opportunity Funds programme can cover the costs that block most families — TOEFL/IELTS fees, application fees, even flight deposits. Competitive, but real. Start by attending a free orientation session.
