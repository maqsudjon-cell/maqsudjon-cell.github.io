How much does IELTS cost in Uzbekistan in 2026?

fees 15 July 2026 · by Maqsudjon Polatov

Key facts

  • Current fee: roughly UZS 2.37 – 2.52 million (centre and format dependent)
  • Academic = General Training — same price
  • Where: IDP and British Council centres, paper or computer
  • Always check the live price on the booking page — fees change without much noise

As of mid-2026, sitting IELTS in Uzbekistan costs roughly 2.37 to 2.52 million so'm, depending on the test centre and format. That's a serious amount of money for most students — which is exactly why the smartest spending decision happens before booking, not after.

What the fee includes

  • All four modules: Listening, Reading, Writing, Speaking
  • One Test Report Form (TRF) — plus a number of free electronic score dispatches to institutions
  • Computer-delivered results typically arrive in 1–5 days; paper-based takes about 13 days

How to pay

Both centres accept card payment online during booking; IDP also lists bank payment via KDB Bank Uzbekistan. Book directly on the official pages — ielts.idp.com/uzbekistan or britishcouncil.uz — and avoid middlemen who charge "service fees" on top.
