Free official IELTS prep — straight from the test makers

resource 16 July 2026 · by Maqsudjon Polatov

Key facts

  • British Council LearnEnglish: free IELTS prep courses and webinars
  • Understanding IELTS: a free structured online course by the British Council
  • IDP's prep hub: sample questions + band-scored model answers
  • Combine with full-length mocks — knowledge without timing practice doesn't move the band

The organisations that own IELTS give away more preparation than most paid courses deliver. The British Council's LearnEnglish platform runs free IELTS courses and webinars; its Understanding IELTS course walks through every section with examiner commentary; IDP's prep hub publishes sample papers and — most valuable — model answers with the band they scored.

A free weekly routine that works

  1. 2 evenings: one module of an official course (technique).
  2. 2 evenings: sample questions from the makers (accuracy).
  3. 1 session: a full-length timed mock (stamina + real band) — that's what we built the hub for.
