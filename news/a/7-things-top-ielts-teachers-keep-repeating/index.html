7 things top IELTS teachers keep repeating

article 2026-07-15
F

We read the blogs and channels of the most-followed IELTS educators — ex-examiners and band 9 teachers — and the same advice keeps appearing. Here are seven points they all agree on.

1. Answer the question, not the topic. Task 2 essays lose bands not for grammar, but for drifting off the exact question asked.

2. In Writing, ideas beat vocabulary. A clear, well-developed argument in simple English scores higher than a weak argument dressed in memorized "academic" phrases.

3. In Speaking, fluency beats accuracy. Hesitating for the perfect word costs more than a small grammar slip. Keep talking; self-correct naturally.

4. In Listening, predict before you hear. Read the questions in the prep time and guess what type of word fits each gap. This single habit is worth half a band.

5. In Reading, answers mostly come in order. For most question types the answers follow the passage order — use it to stop re-reading from the top.

6. Do full tests under real timing. Untimed practice builds knowledge; timed practice builds the score.

7. Review mistakes deeper than you practice. One test fully analysed is worth three tests done and forgotten.

Eng zo'r IELTS ustozlari takrorlaydigan 7 narsa

Eng ko'p kuzatiladigan IELTS ustozlari — sobiq imtihonchilar va band 9 o'qituvchilar — blog va kanallarini o'qib chiqdik, va bir xil maslahat qayta-qayta uchraydi. Mana ular kelishadigan 7 nuqta.

1. Mavzuga emas, savolga javob bering. Task 2 essaylari grammatika uchun emas, aynan berilgan savoldan chetga chiqqani uchun ball yo'qotadi.

2. Writing'da g'oya so'z boyligidan ustun. Oddiy inglizchada aniq va to'liq ochilgan fikr, yodlangan "akademik" iboralarga o'ralgan zaif fikrdan yuqori baholanadi.

3. Speaking'da ravonlik aniqlikdan ustun. Ideal so'zni izlab to'xtab qolish, kichik grammatik xatodan ko'proq ball yeydi. Gapirishda davom eting, tabiiy o'zingizni tuzating.

4. Listening'da eshitishdan oldin bashorat qiling. Tayyorgarlik vaqtida savollarni o'qing va har bo'sh joyga qanday so'z turi mos kelishini taxmin qiling. Shu bitta odat yarim ballga arziydi.

5. Reading'da javoblar asosan tartib bilan keladi. Ko'p savol turlarida javoblar matn tartibiga ergashadi — buni ishlatib, boshidan qayta o'qishni to'xtating.

6. To'liq testlarni real vaqtda toping. Vaqtsiz mashq bilim beradi; vaqt bilan mashq ball beradi.

7. Xatolarni mashqdan chuqurroq tahlil qiling. To'liq tahlil qilingan bitta test, qilinib unutilgan uchta testdan qimmatroq.
