DAAD 2027: Germany's funded master's — most EPOS deadlines fall between August and December

deadline 16 July 2026 · by Maqsudjon Polatov

Key facts

  • DAAD EPOS: funded master's for professionals from developing countries — Uzbekistan eligible
  • Covers: €992/month stipend + insurance + travel (typical package)
  • Deadlines are per programme, mostly August–December 2026
  • Most programmes want ~2 years of work experience and IELTS 6.0–6.5

DAAD — the German Academic Exchange Service — runs the EPOS scholarship line: development-related master's programmes in English or German, with a monthly stipend, health insurance and travel covered. Unlike Chevening or Stipendium Hungaricum, there is no single national deadline: you apply directly to each programme, usually through the university, and most cut-offs land between August and December for the following autumn.

Summer checklist

  1. Open the EPOS programme list on daad.de and shortlist 2–3 courses in your field.
  2. Check each one's language requirement — typically IELTS 6.0–6.5 — and its exact deadline.
  3. Line up two references and your work-experience documents early; German programmes read them closely.
